About CHRISREAL

Shaping Tomorrow's Leaders Since 2015

Our Story

CHRISREAL Educational Complex was founded with a vision to provide world-class education in Kasoa and its environs. We combine academic excellence with strong moral values to develop well-rounded individuals.

Our modern campus, experienced faculty, and child-centered approach have made us one of the fastest-growing schools in the Central Region.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To be a leading institution recognized for academic excellence and character formation.

Mission

To nurture confident, innovative, and morally upright learners who will positively impact society.
