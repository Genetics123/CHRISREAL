Life at CHRISREAL

Beyond Academics

Developing the whole child through a synergy of curriculum, culture, and physical excellence.

Academic Excellence

Our NACCA-based curriculum is blended with high Ghanaian and International standards, delivered in small class sizes for personalized mentorship.

Sports & Athletics

From competitive football and basketball to the discipline of the School Cadet, we nurture physical fitness and a resilient team spirit.

Arts & Creativity

Through music, dance, drama, and cultural showcases, we provide a vibrant stage for constituents to express their unique creative voices.
